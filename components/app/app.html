About

Why choose Remington Construction?

We provide our clients with valuable expertise, high quality service and state-of-the-art technology and materials for all your building maintenance and construction needs. We specialize in commercial, industrial, institutional, educational, medical and multi-unit markets throughout MD, VA and Washington D.C.

Value Engineering

Our project team is highly skilled in finding creative ways to reduce costs while maintaining quality, function, and aesthetics. We design, estimate and self-perform most of the work that yields the best final cost to you, our client.

Service When You Need It

We reply to inquiries promptly! We include personalized service and a timely turnaround into every project we are a part of. Starting with estimating, we give you an accurate understanding of the project costs. We believe that it is our job to not just deliver successful outcomes, but also a smooth, enjoyable process that respects project owners’ investments. Our integrity and professionalism enables us to provide unsurpassed accountability for every client.

Innovation & Sustainability

We embrace the newest technological developments in our industry and offer our clients superior products that make their projects safer, more energy efficient, offer extended life span and provide greater environmental sustainability. Remington is dedicated to becoming the leader in embracing those technologies that help our clients meet and exceed building and energy codes for commercial buildings.

Remington Construction offers a full line of construction and maintenance services for your building’s needs. We place a special emphasis on quality construction and customer service and are 100% committed to client satisfaction and repeat business!
