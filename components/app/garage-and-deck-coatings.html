Resurfacing concrete is an economical and environmentally friendly solution to rejuvenate old concrete surfaces. Concrete deteriorates with age, and environmental factors such as the settling of the substrate, weather, freeze and thaw cycles, salt and many other factors accelerate its wear and shorten its life span. Damaged concrete such as cracks, chips and spalling not only look bad, but opens the surface to water penetration and further damage.

Remington offers a wide range of polyurethane and epoxy traffic coatings that are designed to make concrete decks impermeable to water while offering durability and flexibility that meets the most demanding service conditions. We have the expertise and the experience to creatively support your design ideas and fulfill different requirements of the park garage construction.

The protective coatings we use are formulated for ease of application and environmental safety, in addition to numerous performance benefits. These benefits include carbonation reduction, protection against water, chlorides and chemical ingress, hairline crack sealing, protection against cracking caused by dynamic and thermal movement, and improved light reflectivity and brightness.

Remington offers a full range of elastomeric, crack-bridging waterproofing solutions:

  • Below grade waterproofing
  • Epoxy Injections
  • Route & Seal
  • Joint Caulking
  • Water Testing
