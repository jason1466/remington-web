Location: Leesburg, VA
Scope: 200,000 sq. ft facility

Design -Build project featuring both Plumbing and HVAC work. HVAC - Multiple fan coil units connected through plate-to-plate heat exchangers to chillers and boilers therefore can provide heat and cool simultaneously. Dehumidification systems for both ice rink arenas, RTU units for cooling in dry events. Exhaust, Make-up and Commercial Kitchen grease duct systems. Plumbing - In-ground/above ground sewer and storm piping, hot-cold water distribution and recirculation, fixtures and drains. Hot water heating with storage and buffer tanks, ice melting capability.
